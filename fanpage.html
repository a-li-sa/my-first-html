Bio

Kero Kero Bonito is an indie band that formed in 2011 from London, England. They consist of the singer Sarah and producers Gus and Jamie. "Kero" is the Japanese onomatopoeic word for a frog's croak, but the band's name is otherwise (intentionally) ambiguous.

Since releasing their first mixtape in 2014, Kero Kero Bonito has released two albums, four EPs, and multiple singles.

"Whether exploring rock, experimental or electronic music, their mission is to challenge conformity in pop."
